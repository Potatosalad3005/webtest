Who is Sotau?

SOTAU LIMITED is a private company limited by shares. It is wholly Zambian-owned and professionally managed, aiming to add value to our customers by offering high-quality services. We are a strong force in the market, ensuring customer satisfaction. The company was incorporated in 2013 and began operations in April 2015.

What do we do?

We offer a wide range of services in the construction sector, including:

Civil & Mechanical Engineering

Offering expert consultancy services in construction and development projects to ensure quality outcomes.

Residential Construction

We specialize in building high-quality homes tailored to the needs of our clients.

Commercial Projects

Delivering top-tier commercial construction services for businesses across Zambia.

Machinery & Equipment

We design and build essential infrastructure projects that contribute to national development.

Ceiling & Painting Works

We provide end-to-end project management services ensuring timely and successful completion of projects.
